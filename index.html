What we do

Built for students who want to actually build.

We're a student-led community exploring real-world AI and emerging technologies through workshops, guest speakers, and collaborative projects.

🧠

Learn by doing

Weekly sessions with practical topics like prompt engineering, model evaluation, data visualization, and responsible AI. No fluff — just skills you can use.

🛠

Build together

Team up on beginner-friendly and advanced projects — from chatbots and computer vision to campus-focused tools you can demo in interviews.

🌐

Grow your network

Connect with alumni, researchers, and industry speakers. Share internships, research, and hackathon opportunities with people who actually care about tech.
