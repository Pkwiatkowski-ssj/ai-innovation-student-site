About the club

We’re a student-led community exploring real-world AI and emerging technologies through workshops, guest speakers, and collaborative projects.

Learn by doing

Weekly sessions with practical topics like prompt engineering, model evaluation, data visualization, and responsible AI.

Build together

Team up on beginner-friendly and advanced projects — from chatbots and computer vision to campus-focused tools.

Grow your network

Connect with alumni, researchers, and industry speakers. Share internships, research, and hackathon opportunities.

Upcoming events

Workshops, guest talks, and build nights designed for busy students.
