Prompt Lab

Your AI prompt starter kit.

Stuck on where to begin? Grab a prompt, remix it, and build something. New prompt every week — or generate your own.

Prompt of the Week
RAG • Intermediate
Build a mini-RAG pipeline: ingest 3–5 PDFs, chunk them, embed with a free model, and answer questions with source citations. Measure how often answers reference the right document.

RAG is one of the most in-demand patterns in production AI. Knowing how to build one gives you a real edge in interviews and internship projects.
